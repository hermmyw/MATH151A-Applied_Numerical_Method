(e) In the absence of a known value for Fâ€²(x0) what boundary condition 
T[2](d)(ii)-T[2](d)(iv) would you recommend be used for creating a quadratic 
spline? Explain.

I would use (iii) which has a convergence order of 3. Also, since computing 
the first order one-sided difference results in similar convergence order 
and error as the two-sided difference, choosing one-sided requires less 
computation. (iii) does not need to compute the derivative.
